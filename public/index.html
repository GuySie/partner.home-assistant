What is the Works with Home Assistant Program?

The Works with Home Assistant program is for Manufacturers who want to give their users the best experience in the platform. While community developers can build integrations from available APIs, this program places that responsibility on to the manufacturer in order to keep the best possible integration. For example, the manufacturer will know when API changes are coming and update their integration to support these changes. Our goal is to create a list of devices that "just work" with Home Assistant.

FAQ

What if I post negative feedback or comments?

If the feedback is honest and constructive then you have our thanks and full support. We want our members act honestly with us, our community and our devs. Home Assistant is committed to be community first and building that community up.

Will I get paid as a member of the creator network?

No. Creators are free to monetize their content wherever they operate.

Do I need to pay to be a part of the creator network?

Nope!
